About SCVT

The Scuba Club at Virginia Tech was founded Feb 22, 1965 and since been the center of the dive community in the New River Valley. Based out of Virginia Tech in Blacksburg VA, the SCVT offers training, equipment rental, trip planning, and is an active registered student organization. Room 5 near the pool in War Memorial Gym facilitates normal business of the club during daily gear hours as well as it houses rental equipment, air compressor, and other storage. Annual memberships provide many benefits and is included in all open water certification courses taught by the club. Bring a friend and come out to share our passion for the underwater world!
